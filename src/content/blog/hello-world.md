## Welcome to my first blog post!

I want to use this first post to share why I started this website and how I plan to use it going forward.
As I'm nearing my end of studies in mathematics and computer science, I felt it was time to collect, document and present the projects I've worked on over the past few years.

Here you'll find both my research-related work in machine learning, as well as some personal software projects that grew out of curiosity.

Beyond showcasing some of my projects, I'll also write about papers, ideas and concepts that catch my interest.
I belive that reflecting on concepts and explaining them cleary is one of the best ways to deepen understanding.
Since I am planning to persue a career in academics, this will also help to improve my writing skills.
If this page will additionally reach a few people with similar interests - even better.
